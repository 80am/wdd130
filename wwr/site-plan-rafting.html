Purpose

Overall, the purpose of establishing an online presence for your rafting business is to enhance visibility, attract customers, streamline operations, and ultimately grow your business.

Audience

Our target audience for the water rafting website includes adventurous individuals and groups seeking thrilling outdoor experiences in natural settings. They are typically between 25 to 45 years old, with middle to upper-middle-class financial capabilities, and have a strong interest in adventure sports and nature exploration. Scenario Questions and Answers: Who are the target customers? Target customers are individuals aged 25 to 45, with disposable income for leisure activities, who are passionate about outdoor adventures like water rafting. Describe their life situation (age, financial situation, interests, etc.): They are in the middle to upper-middle class financially, actively seek adrenaline-pumping experiences, enjoy exploring nature, and are part of groups such as friends, families, or adventure clubs. What do they want? They want thrilling experiences, nature immersion, memorable adventures, and opportunities to create lasting memories with friends or family. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? They expect to find detailed information about rafting trips (duration, difficulty levels, safety measures), pricing and packages, safety protocols, customer reviews and testimonials, and easy access to contact and support for inquiries or assistance. By understanding our target audience's characteristics, interests, and expectations, we can tailor our website content to meet their needs effectively and provide a seamless booking experience for their next thrilling water rafting adventure.
